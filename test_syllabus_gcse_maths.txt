GCSE Mathematics Syllabus - Foundation and Higher Tier

Unit 1: Number and Algebra
- Number operations and properties
- Fractions, decimals, and percentages
- Ratio and proportion
- Algebraic expressions and equations
- Sequences and graphs
- Linear equations and inequalities

Unit 2: Geometry and Measures
- Properties of shapes and angles
- Transformations (reflection, rotation, translation)
- Pythagoras' theorem and trigonometry
- Area and volume calculations
- Circle geometry and theorems
- Coordinate geometry

Unit 3: Statistics and Probability
- Data collection and representation
- Measures of central tendency and spread
- Probability theory and tree diagrams
- Cumulative frequency and box plots
- Scatter graphs and correlation
- Sampling methods

Assessment Objectives:
AO1: Use and apply standard techniques (50%)
AO2: Reason, interpret and communicate mathematically (25%) 
AO3: Solve problems within mathematics and other contexts (25%)

Key Topics for Age 14-16:
- Quadratic equations and graphs
- Simultaneous equations
- Indices and surds
- Trigonometric ratios
- Statistical distributions
- Geometric proof
