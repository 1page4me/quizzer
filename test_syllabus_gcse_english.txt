GCSE English Language Syllabus

Paper 1: Explorations in Creative Reading and Writing
Fiction Texts and Creative Writing
- Reading comprehension of literary fiction
- Analysis of language, structure and form
- Creative writing skills and techniques
- Narrative writing and descriptive writing
- Understanding writer's methods and effects

Paper 2: Writers' Viewpoints and Perspectives  
Non-fiction Texts and Transactional Writing
- Comparing writers' ideas and perspectives
- Analysis of persuasive and informative texts
- Transactional writing (letters, articles, speeches)
- Understanding bias, fact and opinion
- Critical evaluation of sources

Speaking and Listening Assessment:
- Individual presentations
- Group discussions and interactions
- Formal speaking skills
- Listening and responding appropriately

Key Skills for Ages 14-16:
- Critical analysis and evaluation
- Inference and interpretation
- Comparison of texts and viewpoints
- Spelling, punctuation and grammar
- Vocabulary development and usage
- Planning and structuring writing

Assessment Objectives:
AO1: Identify and interpret information (15%)
AO2: Explain and analyse language and structure (15%)
AO3: Compare writers' ideas and perspectives (15%)
AO4: Evaluate texts critically (10%)
AO5: Communicate clearly and effectively (20%)
AO6: Use vocabulary, grammar and structure accurately (25%)

Grade Boundaries:
Foundation Tier: Grades 1-5
Higher Tier: Grades 4-9
